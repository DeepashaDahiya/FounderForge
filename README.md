# FounderForge
What This Actually Is FounderForge — Your AI War Room You enter your startup idea. Within seconds, four hyper-specialized AI agents come alive on screen — each with a distinct personality, agenda, and expertise. They don't just give advice. They talk to each other. They disagree. They challenge each other's assumptions. 
The CFO questions the growth hacker's CAC projections. The skeptical investor tears apart the legal advisor's optimism. You watch it unfold in real time like a live boardroom session.
Then at the end, it synthesizes everything into a downloadable war room report — SWOT analysis, go-to-market strategy, financial model, risk assessment, and a pitch deck. All generated. All coherent. All based on your specific idea.
